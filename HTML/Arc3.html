Jujutsu Kaisen Shibuya Incident Arc

The Shibuya Incident Arc sees the villainous plot to trap civilians in Shibuya on Halloween night unfold, leading to Satoru Gojo being sealed in the Prison Realm and a massive, tragic clash between jujutsu sorcerers and cursed spirits that shakes the entire jujutsu society to its core.
